📍 How To Use

World Map Guide

🗺️ Navigating the Map
  • Drag — Click and drag the map to pan around
  • Scroll Wheel — Zoom in and out
  • Click a location dot — Select it to see info on the right panel
  • 1:1 Button — Resets zoom back to normal view
📂 Map View Tabs
  • World — The whole continent/world view
  • Region — A province or major area
  • Local — Your current general area
  • Nearby — Immediate surroundings
  • Area — Room-by-room blueprint view
✈️ Traveling
  • Click a location dot on the map
  • Its name & info appears on the right panel
  • Press "Move To Area" to fast-travel there
  • Press "Open Area" for a detailed room view
🏗️ Building the World
  • Expand — AI generates new nearby locations
  • Add Place — Manually add a new location
  • Free Place — Drag-and-drop a location anywhere
  • Import / Export — Save or load your map data
🎨 Color Legend — What Do The Dots Mean?
🟢 You — Your current location 🔴 Target — Destination or objective 🔵 Station/Dock — Travel hub 🟡 Vehicle/Ship — Transport 🟠 Danger — Hostile area ⚪ Service — Shop or service 🟤 Interior — Inside a building
